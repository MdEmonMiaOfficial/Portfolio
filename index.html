About Me

Bangladesh is a country in South Asia, located in the delta of the Padma (Ganges) and Jamuna (Brahmaputra) rivers. It is one of the most densely populated countries in the world, with a predominantly Muslim population. The capital of Bangladesh is Dhaka. The country has flat plains and is situated on deltas of large rivers flowing from the Himalayas. The official language is Bengali.
