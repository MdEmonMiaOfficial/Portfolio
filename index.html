My Work

Cybersecurity Researcher

My cybersecurity research focuses on emerging threats, attack vectors, and defensive mechanisms. I explore vulnerabilities in web technologies, study malware behavior, and experiment with ethical hacking tools. I regularly document findings and contribute to the infosec community, aiming to make the digital world safer through shared knowledge.

Penetration Testing

I have conducted various penetration testing projects on web applications and network infrastructures. Using tools like Burp Suite, Nmap, and Metasploit, I simulated real-world cyber-attacks to identify vulnerabilities. My goal has always been to help organizations proactively secure their systems before malicious attackers can exploit weaknesses.

Bug Hunting

As an active bug bounty hunter, I have reported numerous vulnerabilities in popular platforms. My focus areas include XSS, SQLi, IDOR, and authentication flaws. I responsibly disclose bugs through platforms like HackerOne and Bugcrowd, contributing to global cybersecurity while enhancing my skills through practical, real-world experience.
